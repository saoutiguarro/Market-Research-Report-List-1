<p><p>The portable multimeter market is divided into two main types: digital multimeters and analog multimeters. Digital multimeters (DMMs) display measurements in numerical form, often featuring advanced functionalities like data logging, auto-ranging, and higher accuracy, making them suitable for professional applications. In contrast, analog multimeters use a needle and dial to show readings, which can be advantageous for observing trends in measurements. While DMMs dominate due to their precision and ease of use, analog multimeters are valued for simplicity and durability in specific contexts.</p></p>
