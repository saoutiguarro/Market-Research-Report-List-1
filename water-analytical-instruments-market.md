<p><p>The Water Analytical Instruments market encompasses various technologies for water quality assessment. Turbidometers measure the cloudiness of water, while floc testers evaluate the settling characteristics of particles. BOD systems determine the biochemical oxygen demand, indicating organic pollution levels. Colorimeters assess light absorption to quantify specific substances, whereas spectrophotometers analyze light absorption across various wavelengths for detailed chemical analysis. Electrochemistry instruments measure electrical properties related to chemical species, and chromatography techniques separate and analyze complex mixtures in water samples.</p></p>
