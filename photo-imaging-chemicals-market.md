<p><p>The photo-imaging chemicals market comprises various compounds essential for photographic processes. Acetic acid serves as a solvent and developer, enhancing image clarity. Cadmium bromide is used in light-sensitive films for high-resolution imaging. Silver bromide is a key component in traditional photography, forming the basis of photographic emulsions. Sodium sulphide is utilized as a reducing agent, aiding in image development. Selenium dioxide functions as a toner, improving image longevity and contrast in photographic prints, making these chemicals pivotal in photography and imaging technologies.</p></p>
