<p><p>The Bismuth Oxide Sputtering Target Market is divided into two main types: rotary and non-rotatable targets. Rotary targets rotate during the sputtering process, allowing for uniform material deposition and increased target utilization, making them ideal for high-volume applications. Non-rotatable targets, on the other hand, remain stationary and are typically used for applications where precision is important but volume is lower. Each type caters to different manufacturing needs based on efficiency, deposition quality, and application requirements.</p></p>
