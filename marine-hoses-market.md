<p><p>The marine hoses market includes expandable hoses and porous or perforated hoses, designed for specific marine applications. Expandable hoses are lightweight, flexible, and ideal for tasks like deck cleaning or water transfer, as they expand under pressure and contract for easy storage. Porous or perforated hoses are used for dewatering, irrigation, or filtration, featuring small holes to allow water to seep through while retaining solids. Both types cater to marine environments, offering durability, efficiency, and adaptability to various operational needs.</p></p>
