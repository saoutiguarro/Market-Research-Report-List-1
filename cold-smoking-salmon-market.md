<p><p>Cold smoking salmon is a traditional preservation method that involves curing fresh salmon fillets with a mixture of salt and sugar before exposing them to smoke at temperatures below 90°F (32°C). This process imparts a rich, smoky flavor while maintaining the fish's tenderness and moisture. The resulting product is celebrated for its delicate taste and is often featured in gourmet dishes, salads, and appetizers.</p><p>The Cold Smoking Salmon Market is experiencing robust growth, driven by increasing consumer demand for premium seafood products and a rising trend towards healthy eating.
