<p><p>The natural flavor extract market comprises two primary types: dry flavor extracts and liquid flavor extracts. Dry flavor extracts are concentrated powders derived from natural sources, often used in applications where moisture content must be minimized, such as baking and seasoning blends. Liquid flavor extracts, on the other hand, are typically solutions of concentrated flavors in liquid form, suitable for beverages, sauces, and marinades. Both types cater to diverse culinary uses, offering consumers various options for enhancing taste and aroma in food and beverages.</p></p>
