<p><p>The Photovoltaic Installation System market is categorized into three main types: rooftop, ground-mounted, and carport installations. Rooftop systems are typically installed on residential or commercial buildings, utilizing otherwise unused space. Ground-mounted systems are set up on land, ideal for larger-scale power generation. Carport installations integrate solar panels into parking structures, providing both shade and energy production.
