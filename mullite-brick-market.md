<p><strong>The Mullite Brick Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Sintered Mullite Brick</li><li>Fused Mullite Brick</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The mullite brick market primarily includes two types: sintered mullite brick and fused mullite brick. Sintered mullite bricks are produced through a firing process, offering high thermal stability and resistance to thermal shock, making them suitable for applications in kilns and furnaces. In contrast, fused mullite bricks are created by melting alumina and silica materials, resulting in enhanced durability and performance under extreme conditions. Both types are widely utilized in refractory applications across industries like steel, cement, and ceramics.</p></p>
