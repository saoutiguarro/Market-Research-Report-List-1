<p><p>The nitrate fertilizers market consists of several key types, including Calcium Ammonium Nitrate (CAN), Ammonium Nitrate (AN), and Calcium Nitrate (CN). CAN combines calcium and ammonium to enhance nutrient retention in soils, making it ideal for various crops. AN is a highly soluble nitrogen source, promoting rapid plant growth, commonly used in cereal crops. Calcium Nitrate provides both calcium and nitrogen, supporting root development and improving soil structure, making it beneficial for fruits and vegetables.</p></p>
