<p><p>The oil film bearing lubricant market is typically segmented into two main types based on viscosity: Type 100 and Type 95. Type 100 lubricants feature a higher viscosity, providing better load-carrying capacity and stability under extreme conditions, ideal for heavy machinery and high-temperature applications. Conversely, Type 95 lubricants have a slightly lower viscosity, allowing for better flow and reduced friction in lighter machinery. Both types are essential for optimizing performance and minimizing wear in various industrial and automotive systems.</p></p>
