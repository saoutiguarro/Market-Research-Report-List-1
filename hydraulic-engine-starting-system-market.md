<p><ul><li>Geared Hydraulic Starter</li><li>Piston Hydraulic Starter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Hydraulic Engine Starting System market encompasses two primary types: geared hydraulic starters and piston hydraulic starters. Geared hydraulic starters utilize a gear mechanism to amplify hydraulic pressure for engine ignition, offering efficient torque and speed. In contrast, piston hydraulic starters function by converting hydraulic energy into mechanical motion through a piston, generating the necessary force to start the engine. Both systems are essential for various applications, including aviation and heavy machinery, where reliable engine starting under challenging conditions is crucial.</p></p>
