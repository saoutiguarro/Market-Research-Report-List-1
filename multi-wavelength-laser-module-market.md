<p><p>The multi-wavelength laser module market is primarily divided into two main types: multimode and single-mode lasers. Multimode lasers support multiple light modes and are ideal for applications requiring high power output and shorter distances, such as data centers and local area networks. In contrast, single-mode lasers transmit light through a single path, offering higher precision and longer distances, making them suitable for long-haul telecommunications and high-speed data transmission. Each type serves distinct applications based on performance and distance requirements.</p></p>
