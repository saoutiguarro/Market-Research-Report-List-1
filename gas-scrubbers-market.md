<p><p>Gas scrubbers are essential devices used to remove pollutants from industrial emissions. The market is primarily divided into two types: wet and dry scrubbers. Wet scrubbers utilize a liquid solution to capture and neutralize gases, effectively removing contaminants like sulfur dioxide and particulate matter. In contrast, dry scrubbers use solid materials, such as activated carbon or lime, to absorb or chemically react with pollutants. Each type serves specific applications and industries, depending on the nature of the pollutants and operational requirements.</p></p>
