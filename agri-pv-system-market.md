<p><p>Agri-PV systems combine agriculture with photovoltaic solar technology, creating two primary market types: Ground and Aquatic Systems. Ground systems utilize land for both farming and solar energy production, optimizing space and enhancing crop yields while generating electricity. Aquatic systems install solar panels on water bodies, reducing evaporation and preventing algae growth, while generating renewable energy. Both systems promote sustainable practices by maximizing land and water resources, contributing to food security and energy efficiency in agricultural settings.</p></p>
