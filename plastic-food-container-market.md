<p><ul><li>PET Plastic</li><li>PE Plastic</li><li>PP Plastic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The plastic food container market is primarily categorized into three types: PET (Polyethylene Terephthalate), PE (Polyethylene), and PP (Polypropylene). PET is widely used for its clarity and recyclability, making it ideal for beverages and ready-to-eat meals. PE is durable and moisture-resistant, commonly used for containers and wraps. PP is noted for its heat resistance and flexibility, suitable for microwavable containers and takeout packaging. Each type offers unique properties, catering to diverse consumer needs in food storage and convenience.</p></p>
