Blowing agents create foams for lightweight applications, and nucleating agents promote uniformity and improve clarity in molded products. Other additives may include colorants and anti-blocking agents, serving specialized functions to meet diverse industry requirements in packaging, automotive, and consumer goods sectors.</p></p>
