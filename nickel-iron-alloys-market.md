<p><ul><li>Controlled Expansion Alloys</li><li>Low Expansion Alloys</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Nickel-iron alloys are specialized materials that exhibit unique thermal expansion properties, primarily categorized into controlled expansion alloys and low expansion alloys. Controlled expansion alloys are designed to maintain a stable coefficient of thermal expansion, making them ideal for precision instruments and applications requiring dimensional stability. Low expansion alloys, on the other hand, offer minimal thermal expansion, suitable for environments with significant temperature fluctuations. Both types find extensive use in aerospace, electronics, and high-precision engineering applications, ensuring reliability and performance.</p></p>
