<p><p>Microchannel Plate Detectors are classified into three main types: Single, Chevron, and Z-Stack. The Single configuration consists of a single layer of microchannels that amplify incident particles efficiently. The Chevron design features two stacked plates arranged in a V-shape, enhancing gain and improving resolution by allowing more precise electron multiplication. The Z-Stack uses multiple plates stacked vertically, offering even greater amplification and linearity, making it suitable for applications requiring high sensitivity and resolution in detecting low levels of light or particles.</p></p>
